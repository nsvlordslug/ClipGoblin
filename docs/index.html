ClipGoblin

Turn your Twitch streams into viral clips

ClipGoblin watches your Twitch VODs, automatically detects the best moments, generates AI-powered captions and titles, and uploads directly to YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram.

⚡

Smart Highlight Detection

Analyzes audio spikes, chat activity, and visual changes to find your best moments automatically. No manual scrubbing required.

✎

AI Captions & Titles

Generate scroll-stopping captions in multiple tones: hype, punchy, observation, or direct quote. Titles that sound like a real creator wrote them.

🎬

Multi-Platform Upload

Connect your YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram accounts. Upload clips directly from the app with one click.

🎨

Subtitle Styles

Speech-to-text subtitles with multiple visual styles. Position them where they look best for vertical or horizontal formats.

☃

Vertical Crop & Facecam

Auto-crop to 9:16 for Shorts and Reels. Smart facecam detection keeps you in frame.

🔒

Your Keys, Your Data

Bring your own API key for AI features. Everything runs locally on your machine. No cloud, no subscriptions, no data collection.

How it works

1
Paste a VOD link Drop in a Twitch VOD URL. ClipGoblin downloads it and starts analyzing.
2
Review detected highlights See a ranked list of the best moments with timestamps, intensity scores, and event tags.
3
Edit and polish Trim timing, add subtitles, pick a caption tone, adjust the crop. Preview everything before export.
4
Export and upload Render your clip and upload to YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram directly from the app.

ClipGoblin is a desktop app for Windows. Currently in development.
