ClipGoblin

Stream → clip → scroll-stopping short. Hands-off.

ClipGoblin pulls your Twitch VODs, fuses chat spikes, audio energy, and Twitch community-clip data to find the moments that actually pop, writes AI captions and titles, and ships them to YouTube and TikTok on a schedule you control — or fully automatically.

The Dashboard — your stream scored, queued, and ready to ship.

🔥

Multi-Signal Detection

Fuses chat spikes, audio energy, scene changes, and Twitch community-clip data to rank the moments real viewers reacted to — not just loud ones.

⚡

Auto-Ship Mode

Opt in and ClipGoblin analyzes, exports, and queues uploads for every new VOD automatically. Wake up to a scheduled drop already in the pipeline.

How it works

1
Connect Twitch One-click OAuth pulls your VOD list. Or paste any public Twitch VOD URL to analyse a stream you don't own.
2
Analyse (or let it run nightly) Chat, audio, scene, and community-clip signals fuse into a ranked highlight list. Each clip gets a viral-score and detected vibe tag.
3
Polish in the editor — or skip it Trim, crop, add subtitles, pick a caption tone. Or enable Auto-Ship and let defaults handle every step for you.
4
Schedule & publish YouTube and TikTok connected in-app. Upload instantly or drip them out across the week with the built-in scheduler.

Heads-up for first-run
  • Windows SmartScreen will warn “Unknown publisher” on first launch — click More info → Run anyway. Not code-signed yet; that'll come with Steam distribution later.
